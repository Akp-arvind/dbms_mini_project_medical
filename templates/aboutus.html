ABOUT Pharmacy Management System

One day, while visiting a medical shop, we encountered a situation where the required medicines were out of stock, and the shopkeeper had to go to the pharmacy to replenish them. This inconvenience sparked an idea: why not establish a management system that directly provides products to medical shops, eliminating the need for them to visit pharmacies for restocking? Subsequently, when tasked with a database management system (DBMS) mini-project, our team decided to implement this idea.
